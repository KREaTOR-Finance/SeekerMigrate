Welcome to SeekerMigrate

SeekerMigrate is infrastructure for professional teams. It helps you ship Solana Mobile compatible dApps without storing private keys and without guessing about compliance.

  • You remain responsible for custody, compliance, and production rollout decisions.
  • Never paste private keys or seed phrases into this app.
  • All mainnet flows should be validated in staging before public release.
